Terms of Service - Managed Service (Alpha)

Effective Date: February 11, 2026

Version: Alpha 1.0

1. Service Description

SolvencyAI Managed Service ("Service") is a custody-based treasury management service. Users deposit USDC (Solana network), and SolvencyAI deploys capital to DeFi lending protocols (currently Kamino Finance) to generate yield.

This is NOT:

  • A bank or financial institution
  • An investment fund or registered advisor
  • FDIC insured or government backed
  • A guaranteed return product

2. Alpha Phase Status

This service is in ALPHA testing with the following constraints:

  • Maximum 10 users
  • Maximum $200 per user
  • Maximum $2,000 total value locked
  • Experimental features and processes
  • Manual operations (not fully automated)
  • Learning and iterating in real-time

By using this service, you acknowledge that:

  • You could lose your entire deposit

3. Key Risks

3.1 Custody Risk

SolvencyAI controls the wallet holding your funds. "Not your keys, not your coins" applies here. You are trusting us with custody of your assets.

3.2 Protocol Risk

Your funds are deployed to third-party DeFi protocols (currently Kamino Finance). These protocols could:

  • Be exploited by hackers
  • Suffer smart contract bugs
  • Experience liquidity crises
  • Cease operations

While Kamino has $400M+ TVL and multiple audits, no protocol is 100% safe.

3.3 Operational Risk

We could make mistakes, including:

  • Incorrect deposits or withdrawals
  • Loss of private keys
  • Failure to rebalance properly
  • Calculation errors in yield reporting

3.4 Regulatory Risk

Cryptocurrency regulations are evolving. This service could:

  • Be deemed illegal in your jurisdiction
  • Face government seizure or shutdown
  • Require compliance measures that affect withdrawals

3.5 Liquidity Risk

Withdrawals require 48-72 hours because we must unwind DeFi positions. In extreme market conditions, unwinding could take longer or incur losses.

3.6 APY Variability

Target APY is 4-6% but actual returns may be higher or lower based on:

  • DeFi market conditions
  • Protocol utilization rates
  • Rebalancing costs
  • Gas fees

4. Coverage Policy

4.1 Emergency Fund

SolvencyAI maintains an emergency fund currently set at $500 (25% of Phase 1 TVL cap).

4.2 What We Cover

  • Operational Errors (Our Fault): 100% coverage up to emergency fund balance
  • Example: We send funds to wrong address, lose keys, miscalculate yield

4.3 Partial Coverage

  • Protocol Exploits: 10% of total user deposits covered from emergency fund
  • Remaining losses: Shared proportionally among all users

4.4 No Coverage

  • Market losses (stablecoins shouldn't have these, but it's possible)
  • Regulatory seizure
  • Your wallet being compromised
  • Losses beyond emergency fund balance

5. Fees

5.1 Management Fee

20% of earned yield

  • Charged only on profits, not principal
  • Calculated weekly
  • Deducted automatically from your position

Example: Deposit $200, earn $10 yield → You receive $8, we receive $2

5.2 Other Fees

  • Deposit Fee: None
  • Normal Withdrawal Fee: None
  • Emergency Withdrawal (<6 hours): 5% of withdrawn amount

6. Withdrawal Process

6.1 Normal Withdrawal

  1. DM @SolvencyAI on Twitter with withdrawal request
  2. We confirm and begin unwinding DeFi position
  3. 48-72 hours processing time
  4. USDC sent to your original deposit address
  5. Yield settled (your 80%, our 20%)

6.2 Emergency Withdrawal

  • Available for urgent situations only
  • 6-hour processing (best effort)
  • 5% fee applies
  • May require taking DeFi position loss if markets are illiquid

6.3 Withdrawal Delays

In the following scenarios, withdrawals may be delayed:

  • Multiple users withdraw simultaneously (>50% of TVL)
  • DeFi protocol has liquidity issues
  • Technical issues on Solana network
  • Regulatory investigation or legal hold
