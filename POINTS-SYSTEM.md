## How It Works

## Why This Works

**For users:**
- Upside potential without speculation
- Encourages longer holds
- Rewards genuine participation

**For us:**
- Build community without token complexity
- Time to figure out tokenomics
- Avoid regulatory issues
- Focus on product now

**For later:**
- Retroactive rewards = fair distribution
- Community ownership earned, not airdropped
- Governance by users who actually used product
