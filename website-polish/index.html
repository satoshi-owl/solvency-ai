The Future is AgentFi

"In 2030, there will be millions of autonomous agents. Each one needs a treasury."

Today's agents are shackled by human dependency. They run out of API credits. They can't pay for compute. They halt mid-task, waiting for humans to manually fund them.

Solvency AI was born at the Colosseum hackathon to solve this exact problem. We asked: What if agents could manage their own treasuries? What if idle capital earned yield while standing by for operational needs?
