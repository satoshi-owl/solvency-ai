## Logo Concept: "The Sentinel"

**Design:**
- Geometric "S" constructed from flowing curves
- Two subtle gold dots create "watchful" symbolism (owl requirement)
- NOT illustrated - architectural abstraction
- Professional enough for Bloomberg terminals

**Owl Reference:**
- Satisfied through geometric dots, not cartoon illustration
- "Watchful" intelligence suggested, not depicted
- Institutional viewer: "Nice design accents"
- Informed viewer: "Clever owl reference"

**Scalability:**
- Minimum size: 32px (favicon)
- Maximum size: Unlimited (vector SVG)
- All details visible at recommended sizes

## Voice & Tone

### Institutional (V3)

**Before (V1/V2):**
> "Your bot woke you up at 3am because it ran out of API credits."

**After (V3):**
> "Autonomous agents require sustainable infrastructure for API access and operational costs."

**Principles:**
1. Statements over questions
2. "Agents" not "bots"
3. "Infrastructure" not "product"
4. Specific numbers (6-9% APY (target), 1:1 backing)
5. No hype language or crypto slang
