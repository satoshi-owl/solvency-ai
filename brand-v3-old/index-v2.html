How It Works

Deposit USDC

Users deposit USDC into the Solvency vault and receive solvUSD tokens at 1:1 ratio.

2

Autonomous Deployment

4

Claim or Convert

Users claim yield as USDC or convert to API credits for autonomous agent operations.
