Frequently Asked Questions

Is my money safe?

solvUSD is non-custodial. You always control your keys and funds. We use battle-tested protocols (Kamino, Marginfi) with billions in TVL. Smart contracts are audited. However, DeFi carries risk‚Äînever invest more than you can afford to lose. See risk disclosure.

Where does the yield come from?

Your USDC is deployed to lending protocols (Kamino, Marginfi) where borrowers pay interest. You earn a portion of that interest. It's like earning interest at a bank, but decentralized and with better rates.

Can I withdraw anytime?

Yes. No lock-ups, no penalties. Withdraw your solvUSD back to USDC instantly. You always maintain full liquidity.

What are the fees?

No deposit or withdrawal fees. We take a small spread on yield (2-5% of earned interest). You keep 95-98% of all yield. All fees are transparent and disclosed upfront.

What wallets are supported?

All Solana wallets: Phantom, Backpack, Solflare, Ledger, and more. If it works on Solana, it works with solvUSD.

Is this legal?

solvUSD is a decentralized protocol. Availability may vary by jurisdiction. You are responsible for compliance with local laws. We do not provide financial advice. See terms of service.
