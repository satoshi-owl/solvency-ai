## ğŸ”¤ Typography: Clean, Modern, Professional

### Font Stack
```
-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'SF Pro Display', 'Segoe UI', system-ui, sans-serif
```

### Why System Fonts?
1. **Performance**: Zero load time, instant rendering
2. **Familiarity**: Users trust what they recognize
3. **Professionalism**: Apple, Microsoft, and Google use these fonts for enterprise products
4. **Consistency**: Looks native on every device

### Typography Scale
- **H1**: 56px, -1.5px letter-spacing (commanding presence)
- **H2**: 40px, -1px letter-spacing (section headers)
- **Body**: 15-18px (readable, professional)
- **Labels**: 13-14px uppercase with increased letter-spacing (luxury detail)

### Key Decisions
- **Negative letter-spacing** on large headings (modern, tight, expensive-looking)
- **Increased letter-spacing** on small labels (classic luxury technique)
- **Font weights**: 300 (light), 500 (medium), 600 (semibold) only â€” no extremes

## ğŸ“ Layout: Whitespace = Luxury

### Key Principles

### Section Structure
1. **Hero**: 120px top padding, centered, maximum whitespace
2. **Stats**: 80px padding, clean grid, large numbers

4. **Trust**: Navy background section (breaks up page, adds depth)
5. **CTA**: 100px padding, centered, simple
6. **Footer**: Comprehensive but clean, 60px padding

### Why Whitespace Matters
- **Luxury brands** use whitespace to convey value (Apple, Rolex, high-end banks)
- **Cramped layouts** feel cheap and overwhelming
- **Generous spacing** signals "we have nothing to hide, take your time"
