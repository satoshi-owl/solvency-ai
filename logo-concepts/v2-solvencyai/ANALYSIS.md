## RECOMMENDED PRIMARY CHOICE

# üèÜ FINALIST 1: Dual Core

### Why This Wins for SolvencyAI Company Brand

**1. Perfect Conceptual Alignment**  
The dual circles literally represent **Solvency** + **AI**‚Äîthe two core elements of the company name and mission. The intersection shows how they unite to create something new.

**2. Company-Level Identity (Not Product)**  
This logo is broad enough to represent the entire company across multiple products:
- Current: solvUSD token
- Future: Other agent treasury products
- All: Unified under SolvencyAI company brand

**3. Institutional Excellence**  
This design could sit beside logos from Goldman Sachs, BlackRock, or JPMorgan. It has institutional gravitas while feeling modern and tech-forward.

**4. Unforgettable Simplicity**  
Two overlapping circles. That's it. You see it once in a pitch deck, you remember it forever. Perfect for building company brand awareness.

**5. Infinite Scalability**  
Pure geometry means perfect rendering at:
- 16px (favicon, mobile apps)
- 64px (social media, standard usage)
- 500px+ (presentations, marketing)
- Billboard size (investor events, conferences)

**6. Timeless Foundation**  
Based on the vesica piscis, a geometric form used for thousands of years. Not trendy. Not dated. Eternal.

**7. Unique in Fintech/AI Space**  
- No other AI company uses this
- No other fintech company uses this
- Clean, professional, distinctive

**8. Works With or Without Wordmark**  
- Standalone: Recognizable symbol
- With "SolvencyAI": Complete company lockup
- Flexibility for all use cases

### Implementation for SolvencyAI

**Primary Usage:**  
Dual core mark + "SolvencyAI" wordmark  
(Light weight typography, AI emphasized)

**Icon-Only Usage:**  
Dual circles at 16px, 32px, 64px for apps

**Color Recommendations:**  
- Primary: Deep navy (#0A1128) or black
- Accent: Electric blue for "AI" emphasis
- Backgrounds: White, dark, or gradient

**Typography:**  
"Solvency" (light weight 300) + "AI" (medium weight 500)
